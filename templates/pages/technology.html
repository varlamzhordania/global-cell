Our Technology

At Global Cellular Partners, we're at the forefront of innovation in telecommunications. Our specially developed technology utilizes unused Android devices as a tool to generate income from local calls, revolutionizing the way international communication is handled.

How Our Technology Works

1. Client Connection : Our clients from all over the world connect to our VoIP servers and dial an international number to a specific country.
2. Server Routing : Our servers intelligently route the call to a specific Android device in our digital cellular partners network. This Android device is located in the country where the international number belongs.
3. Local Call Establishment : The local Android device that receives the call from our server then dials the local number and creates a digital audio call between our client and our local Global Cellular Partner Android.
